Our Story

Born on Kampala's Seven Hills

OTOKA was founded by Otwiine Olweny and Mark Lui — two runners who believed that Kampala's running culture deserved its own voice. We make gear that moves with you, from the red dirt roads of Kololo to the starting line of your next race.
