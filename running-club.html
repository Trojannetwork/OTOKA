A community that moves together.

The OTOKA Running Club is a space for every runner in Kampala — from first-timers lacing up for the first time, to veterans chasing new PRs. We're building a crew that shows up before sunrise, runs the hills, and supports each other every step of the way.

Community runs, training sessions, events, and more — all built around the same energy that drives OTOKA. No egos, no barriers. Just runners, just movement.

We're getting everything ready behind the scenes. Stay tuned — and if you want to be the first to know when the club launches, drop your email below.

What to Expect

Weekly community runs through Kampala's most iconic routes. Training tips, gear testing, and first access to OTOKA drops. A crew that shows up — rain or shine, 5 AM or 5 PM.

Whether you're training for your first 5K or your next marathon, there's a place for you here.
