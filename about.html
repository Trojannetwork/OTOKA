Our Story

Born on the seven hills of Kampala. Built for runners, by runners.

The Spark

OTOKA was founded by Otwiine Olweny and Mark Lui — two runners who saw a gap between the raw energy of Kampala's running scene and the gear available to the athletes powering it.

Every morning, before the sun breaks over Lake Victoria, runners fill the streets of Kololo, Nakasero, and beyond. They run the hills. They run the red dirt. They run because it's who they are. OTOKA exists to give that community a brand that speaks their language.

Our name draws from the sprint-bar mark — that universal symbol of speed, motion, and momentum. Every piece we make is designed to move with you, whether you're chasing a PR or chasing the sunrise.

What We Stand For

Performance First

Every seam, every panel, every fabric is chosen to help you move faster and feel better. No compromises.

Built in Kampala

Our city shapes everything we do. The hills, the heat, the community — it's all in the DNA of our gear.

Sustainable Speed

We use recycled materials wherever possible. Running gear that respects the planet we run on.
